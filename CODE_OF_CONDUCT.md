# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors and community members.

## Our Standards

- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Be respectful of differing opinions and experiences
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Show empathy towards others

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are unacceptable:
- Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language
- Trolling or insulting/derogatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing private information without permission
- Other conduct considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Reporting Violations

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the project maintainers.

## Enforcement

Violations of this code of conduct may result in temporary or permanent removal from the community.
